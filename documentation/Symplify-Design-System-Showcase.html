B — Background
Admitted for acute MI 48 hours ago. History of HTN, DM2, hyperlipidemia. Heparin drip at 1000u/hr. Last troponin 2.4 (trending down).
A — Assessment
Hemodynamically stable but concerning BP elevation and mild desaturation. May be developing fluid overload post-procedure. Pain controlled 3/10.
R — Recommendation
Request cardiology consult for BP management. Consider diuretic. Continuous SpO₂ monitoring. Repeat troponin in 6 hours. Reassess fluid balance.

⚡ Moderate Interaction: Metformin + Lisinopril
May increase risk of hypoglycemia and lactic acidosis. Monitor renal function and glucose levels.
